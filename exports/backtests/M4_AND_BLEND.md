# Model M4 (Multi-Timeframe Trend) + 60/40 M3+M4 Blend

_Window: May 2023 → May 2026. Universe: Nifty 100 (pseudo by ADV)._

## How M4 Works

```
ENTRY (every quarter — Jan / Apr / Jul / Oct first trading day):
  Rank N100 stocks meeting ALL 4 trend filters:
    1. Daily close > 50-EMA       (short-term uptrend)
    2. Daily 50-EMA > 200-EMA     (medium-term uptrend)
    3. Weekly close > 50-EMA      (weekly trend confirmation)
    4. Pick top-5 by EMA50 / EMA200 ratio (strongest trend strength)

HOLD: full quarter (~90 days)
EXIT: next quarter rebalance OR drop-out of top-5
POSITIONS: 5 concurrent, equal-weight (~₹2L each at ₹10L allocation)
REBALANCE: quarterly (4×/yr)
NO stop-loss, NO profit target — pure quarterly trend ride
```

**Driver:** multi-timeframe trend confirmation. Filters out half the candidates pure 60d momentum (M3) would pick — gives fewer but higher-quality picks. Correlation with M3 = +0.25 (low).
