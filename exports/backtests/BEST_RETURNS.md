## How the 8% Trailing Stop Works

```
On every trading day:
  peak = max(peak_so_far, today_high)
  if today_close <= peak * 0.92:  # 8% drawdown from peak
      EXIT at today_close (Stop hit)
      free up slot for next month's rebalance to pick rank-2
```
Two exit paths:
1. **Rotation exit (Target/Stop hit on 1st of month)** — when stock drops out of top-5 ranking
2. **Trailing-SL exit (Stop hit any day)** — when stock retraces 8% from its post-entry peak

## Caveats

- **Lookahead bias**: universe is 2026-05-13 N100 retroactive across 2023-2025. Real walk-forward live numbers will be lower.
- **Concurrency quirk**: max=1 means 8% trail frees slot for next-best entry. Improvement partly comes from this slot-rotation, not pure SL alpha.
- **Daily-OHLC sim**: real intraday vol triggers 8% SL more often than daily close-only. Live will have more trades + slippage.
- **Realistic live expectation**: 35-50% CAGR after slippage + STT + STCG, compared to +72%/yr backtest.
