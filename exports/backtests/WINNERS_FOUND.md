## What unifies them

All three are **monthly momentum rotation** variants — same engine, different knobs:
- Rank stocks by 60-day return
- Hold top-N (5/7) equal-weight
- Cap concurrent (1/3)
- Rebalance monthly (1st of month)
- No SL/target, hold until rotation removes stock

## Why momentum rotation succeeds where 17 prior phases failed

1. **Continuous adaptation**: Monthly rebalance follows regime shifts. EMA crossover/ORB are static rules.
2. **Universe-agnostic**: Doesn't care if leaders are large/mid/small cap. Picks current leaders.
3. **No SL whipsaw**: Holds positions until ranking changes. EMA SL exits at noise.
4. **Mechanical**: No discretion. No false-alarm filters needed — the ranking is the filter.
