Strategy Overview
What is the 8-21 EMA Strategy?

A pure technical analysis swing trading strategy that identifies high-probability entry points by combining exponential moving averages with momentum oscillators and Fibonacci targets.

Core Components:
Power Zone (Trend Identification)

8 EMA > 21 EMA: Confirms bullish trend when 8-day EMA crosses above 21-day EMA

The "Power Zone" indicates strong momentum when price stays above both EMAs.

DeMarker Oscillator (Entry Timing)

DeMarker < 0.30: Identifies oversold pullbacks within uptrend

Ranges from 0 to 1. Values below 0.30 signal strong buy opportunities (oversold).

Fibonacci Extensions (Profit Targets)

Targets: 127.2%, 161.8%, 200% extensions from recent swing low

Take partial profits at each Fibonacci level for optimal risk/reward.

Risk Management
  • Stop Loss: Below 21 EMA or recent swing low (5-7%)
  • Position Size: Max 5% per trade
  • Time Frame: 5-14 day holds (swing trading)
  • Win Rate Target: 55-65% with 2:1 R/R ratio
